Voting Rules

  1. All voters must be registered with a valid Citizenship ID.
  2. Minimum age requirement is 18 years.
  3. One voter, one vote per election.
  4. Voting must be completed within the designated time frame.
  5. No sharing of login credentials is allowed.
  6. Any attempt to manipulate votes will result in disqualification.
  7. Voters must reside in the jurisdiction they are voting for.
  8. All personal information must be accurate and up-to-date.
  9. Voting is confidential and secure.
  10. Contact support for any technical issues during voting.
